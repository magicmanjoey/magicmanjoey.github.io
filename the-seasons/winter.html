The Seasons

Movement of Winter

Description

This movement begins at midnight on New Year's Day. The piano part of "New Year's Clock Theme" represents the 12 chimes of a clock ringing in the new year. Next, "Snowflakes" describes light flurries at first, but, as the melody goes into lower octaves, the snow gets heavier and is collecting on the ground. The seemingly out-of-place "Silent Night" shows the overall peace and quiet of a snow-filled winter night. It also explores the beauty of the scenery covered with a layer of white. Finally, the mood shifts and the listener finds themself in the middle of a powerful storm. The excerpt from "Hurricane" shows the immense power of nature, through storms, tornadoes, hurricanes, and many other events. The dissonant chords of this selection illustrate the destruction that nature leaves behind through its acts. However, the beginning of The Movement of Spring exposes the beauty and calmness nature provides.
