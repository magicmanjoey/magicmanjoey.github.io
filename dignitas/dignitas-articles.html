The Governing Articles of Dignitas

        On this day, the twenty-fourth of February, year of our Lord: 2016, the Governing Articles of the non territorial Republic of Dignitas were first drafted by her leaders:

President Hayden Blodgett

Chief Judiciary Joseph Steele  

President Sanctor Praepositus Kayla Parker

Speaker of the House Graym Brown

In order to establish peace, create prosperity, promote gentility, reinstitute liberty, preserve justice, and uphold the name of Dignity for all citizens of the Great Republic, under the Almighty God, are written the Governing Articles of Dignitas.


Article I: The Legislature

        SECTION I: The Legislative Congress shall consist of two houses: The House of Representatives and the Senate. Both houses are elected by the people. There shall be a superior Legislature, named by the elected Congressmen of both houses. This superior Legislature shall be named President Sanctor Praepositus and shall reside with the Senate. If there is ever a time where the President Praepositus cannot fulfill his duties, the Congress shall elect a successor. If there is ever a time where the President Praepositus is has committed crimes against the nation or the office in which he holds, he is subject to impeachment by bi-branch concurrence or a joint Executive-Judicial order.

        Impeachment shall be conducted in the following manner: The House of Representatives shall propose the impeachment. After an approval by the house, it shall move to the senate where it is voted under secret ballot. Once this is done, the proposal is sent to the executive and judicial branch where only one approval is required to impeach. If the Senate, by the house, is not seen fit to vote on an impeachment, the proposal will be taken to the Executive and Legislative branch where a concurrent decision must be made in order to impeach. In order for the latter system to be instituted, the House must decide upon the condition of the Senate before bringing the vote elsewhere. A failed attempt in the Senate will halt the impeachment and it shall not be retried again within the present term of office.


Article II: The Executive

SECTION I: The executive branch shall consist of a head and a body. In order to hold the office, you must meet the following qualifications: Naturalized Citizen, Must uphold the Name of Dignitas without conviction of crimes against the Republic.

The President may be impeached by means of the House of Representatives where it must be approved with a two-thirds majority. From the house it goes to the Senate where there must be another two-thirds majority to impeach. Once the impeachment is final, it is up to the Supreme Court to conduct a trial and remove the President from office. In this case, the Vice President will take the office.
